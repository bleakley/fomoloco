FOMO Loco!

🚀🤑💎

This is a drop in multiplayer stock-trading game that uses its own server! It was made for the Brackeys jam but it is hosted at fomolo.co.

Play with your friends! If you start a game at the same time, it is very likely that you will play together. Buy low and sell high! Unlock secret upgrades! And remember- apes are stronger together! 🐵🐵🐵
